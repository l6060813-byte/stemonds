The Company

Where materials science
meets the next frontier.

Stemonds is a team of engineers from Stanford and UCLA united by a single conviction — thermal is one of the most challenging and consequential frontiers in advanced materials. AI chips are getting faster. The heat they generate is getting harder to manage. Diamond — the world's best thermal conductor — is finally getting the attention it deserves.


We grow lab-grown diamond and engineer it to exact specifications for US clients and research institutions. Beyond thermal management, diamond's unique combination of hardness, wide bandgap, optical transparency, and quantum properties makes it one of the most versatile advanced materials ever studied — and we are only beginning to explore what it can do.

Applications

Where diamond
makes the difference.

AI Datacenters

As AI accelerators push the limits of power density, conventional cooling materials struggle to keep pace. Diamond's exceptional thermal properties enable next-generation thermal solutions for the most demanding compute environments.

Electric Vehicles

Power electronics in next-generation EVs operate at extreme frequencies and temperatures. Diamond's thermal and electrical properties open new possibilities for more efficient, compact, and reliable drivetrain systems.

Space Systems

Space demands materials that perform under conditions no ground-based environment can fully replicate. Diamond's exceptional stability and thermal properties make it a compelling choice for next-generation commercial space applications.

Quantum

Diamond's atomic structure makes it one of the most promising host materials for quantum applications — a frontier we are only beginning to explore.

Why Diamond Wins

No material comes close.

Diamond outperforms every competing material across the properties that matter most wherever thermal performance is critical.

Unmatched Heat Transfer

The highest thermal conductivity of any bulk material — far exceeding conventional options used in power electronics today.

Extreme Temperature Stability

Maintains structural integrity and performance across the extreme temperature ranges encountered in space, automotive, and industrial environments.

Minimal Weight Penalty

Delivers superior thermal performance at a fraction of the weight of conventional metal-based cooling solutions — critical for aerospace and EV applications.

Zero Electrical Interference

An exceptional electrical insulator, enabling thermal management directly integrated into high-voltage power electronics without risk of short circuits.
