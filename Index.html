Who We Are

Built for the limits
of what's possible

Stemonds is a team of engineers united by a single conviction: the most important material breakthroughs of the coming decade will be thermal ones. As AI chips grow more powerful, EV powertrains more demanding, and spacecraft more capable — heat becomes the limiting factor.


We grow lab-grown diamond and deliver it to US clients and research institutions who need the most thermally capable material on Earth, engineered to their exact specifications.

Where We Work

Wherever the problem
is heat.

Diamond's properties open doors across many fields. Below are areas where we are actively engaged — but the material's potential goes further.

AI Datacenters

As AI accelerators push the limits of power density, conventional cooling materials struggle to keep pace. Diamond's exceptional thermal properties enable next-generation thermal solutions for the most demanding compute environments.

Electric Vehicles

Power electronics in next-generation EVs operate at extreme frequencies and temperatures. Diamond's thermal and electrical properties open new possibilities for more efficient, compact, and reliable drivetrain systems.

Space Systems

Space imposes thermal extremes that no conventional material can handle alone. Diamond's stability across wide temperature swings, radiation hardness, and thermal management capability make it a compelling material for mission-critical aerospace applications.

Quantum Sensing & Computing

Diamond is uniquely positioned as a host material for quantum technologies. Its atomic-level defect structures enable highly sensitive sensing and qubit functionality — opening doors to quantum applications across science, navigation, and computing.

Why Diamond Wins

No material comes close.

Diamond outperforms every competing material across the properties that matter most wherever thermal performance is critical.

Thermal Conductivity

The highest thermal conductivity of any bulk material — far exceeding conventional options used in power electronics today.

Thermal Stability

Maintains structural integrity and performance across the extreme temperature ranges encountered in space, automotive, and industrial environments.

Mass Efficiency

Delivers superior thermal performance at a fraction of the weight of conventional metal-based cooling solutions — critical for aerospace and EV applications.

Electrical Insulation

An exceptional electrical insulator, enabling thermal management directly integrated into high-voltage power electronics without risk of short circuits.
